Roberto Batong

The man I will always regret not spending my time with

Here's a timeline of My Grandpa's life:

1956

Born in Guinzadan, Bauko, Mountain Province

1963

Attented Guinzadan Elementary School

1969

Graduated Elementary and entered Guinzadan National High School

1973

MArries wife of 46 years until his early passing. Had to stop school during this time at 4th year high school

1990

Went to Baguio and tried his shot at the vegetable business

1992

Went abroad to Malaysia as the business wasn't garnering enough money

1998

Went back to philippines after 6 years abroad.

1999

Went back to abroad in taiwan not long after coming home to continue working.

2002

Went back to philippines and built a house in La Trinidad

2003

The house was already built and the first floor was made a sari-sari store where he now stayed forever.

2019

Dies at the age of 63
